about

I am passionate about computers since I was 10 years old. I've always liked to tinker with anything that had a circuit board, trying to figure out how it worked.

I have some basic knowledge of Python, which is very useful to write scripts and mini software for my own needs.

And I'm currently learning web development (this web site does not reflect my level of learning at all, I just wanted an extremely simple and minimalist design). I currently don't have a portfolio to present my few projects, it will happen soon.

On the hardware side, I'm very attracted by servers. I acquired my first server this year (2022), a Thinkcentre M700 with an i3-6100, 8GB of RAM and a 250gb SSD where I currently host Jellyfin for movies and series, a Samba server to facilitate data transfer between my different devices. I'm planning bigger projects for this little beast.
